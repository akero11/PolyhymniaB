Polyhymnia

Polyhymnia, (Πολυύμνια; "the one of many hymns")

Muse of sacred poetry, sacred hymn, dance, and eloquence as well as agriculture and pantomime.

Polyhymnia is also sometimes credited as being the Muse of geometry and meditation.
